About This Calculator

This Carbon Footprint Calculator helps you understand your daily environmental impact and provides personalized suggestions for reducing your carbon emissions.

Features:

  • Calculate emissions from transportation, diet, and energy use
  • Compare your footprint to global averages
  • Get personalized reduction suggestions
  • Track your progress over time
  • Statistical analysis of your habits

All calculations are based on established emission factors from EPA, IPCC, and other credible sources.

How It Works
Calculation Method

Your carbon footprint is calculated using scientifically-backed emission factors:

Transportation
  • Gas car: 0.4 kg CO₂/mile
  • Electric car: 0.15 kg CO₂/mile
  • Hybrid car: 0.25 kg CO₂/mile
  • Public transport: 0.1 kg CO₂/mile
  • Flights: 0.2-0.25 kg CO₂/mile
Diet
  • Meat meal: 7 kg CO₂
  • Vegetarian meal: 3 kg CO₂
  • Vegan meal: 1.5 kg CO₂
Energy
  • Electricity: 0.5 kg CO₂/kWh
Suggestions Algorithm

Our algorithm analyzes your highest-impact categories and provides weighted suggestions based on potential CO₂ reduction. Higher-impact suggestions are prioritized to maximize your environmental benefit.
